SPIDERMAN

Spider-Man is a Marvel Comics character, also known as Peter Parker, who was bitten by a radioactive spider during a field trip at Oscorp Industries, granting him superhuman powers. These powers include incredible agility and the ability to cling to walls, and he uses mechanical "web-shooters" to swing between buildings and ensnare criminals. He is a heroic figure who uses his abilities to fight crime and protect people, often balancing his superhero life with his personal struggles and responsibilities.
